About the Developer

Hello! I'm Sarthak Sharma, a passionate game developer and designer based in Satna, Madhya Pradesh, India. With a deep love for creating interactive experiences, I've dedicated my career to crafting engaging games that bring joy to players worldwide.

This Flappy Bird recreation is one of my projects aimed at preserving classic gaming experiences while adding my own creative touch. I believe in the power of simple yet challenging gameplay that keeps players coming back for more.

When I'm not coding or designing games, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, and sharing my knowledge through my blog posts.
